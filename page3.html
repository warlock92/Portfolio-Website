Projects

Harmonic Analysis and Mitigation for Metro Rail Power Distribution Network

Sep 2015- May 2016

Masters Thesis project involved complete analysis and mitigation of power quality issues using simulation softwares ETAP and PSCAD, Detailed study was made comparing traditional traction systems and evolving Co-Phase traction system. Also analysed existing railway power conditioners and hybrid power conditioners for traction applications.

Cargo Ground Build-up System

Feb 2015- June 2016

Project involved developing a Cargo Handling Vehicle for C-130 Hercules aircraft especially in un-prepared terrains catering to its cargo handling needs in disaster struck areas and making the vehicle transportable along with the cargo in the aircraft itself.

Automatic Combination Module for Power Sources

May 2015- Feb 2016

Designed a mechanism to combine the power from solar panels with available batteries and Diesel Generators to feed the load in efficient way. Learned a lot about grid integration issues and problems to be solved in that area.

Optimal Design for Non-Solenoidal Operation of LCV Starter Motors

Aug 2013- May 2014

Bachelor's Thesis involved An innovative cost effective design for replacing solenoids in starter motors thereby preventing battery drain and improving battery life which inturn reduces carbon footprint.This resulted in a patent.
